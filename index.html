Tic Tac Toe: Play Against AI



"Tic Tac Toe: Play Against AI" is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid with their designated symbols (X or O). The objective of the game is to be the first to achieve a line of three of their symbols horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, or to fill the entire grid without achieving a winning line, resulting in a draw.
